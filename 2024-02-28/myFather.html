My Father

My father, a laborer, met an untimely death due to an electric shock when I was a child. Despite his hardships, he was loyal and showered me with affection, bringing me ice cream and pocket money. His sudden departure left a void, but his love and teachings remain my guiding light. Despite his occupation as a laborer, my father's loyalty and affection towards me were unwavering. Tragically, his life was cut short by an electric shock when I was a child. Yet, he always made time for me, bringing small joys like ice cream and pocket money. His sudden absence left a lasting impact, but his love continues to shape who I am today.In my heart, my father will always remain a symbol of strength, kindness, and unwavering love. Though his time with me was cut short, his impact on my life is everlasting. And as I journey through life, I know that he watches over me from above, guiding me with his gentle spirit and eternal love.
